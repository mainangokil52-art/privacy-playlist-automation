Privacy Policy – Playlist Automation

Last updated: 2026/02/22

Playlist Automation ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information.

### 1. Information We Collect

Playlist Automation does not collect personal data.

The app may access:

• Audio files you select
• Folder paths you choose
• App settings stored locally

This data is used only to generate playlists and export mixes.

We do NOT upload your files to any server.

### 2. Storage Access

The app requests storage permission to:

• Read audio files you select
• Save generated mixes to your Music folder

All files remain on your device.

### 3. Internet Access

The app may use internet connection to:

• Check for app updates
• Load ads (Google AdMob)

No personal data is collected from this process.

### 4. Ads

Playlist Automation may display ads provided by Google AdMob.

Google may collect anonymous usage data according to their policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 5. Data Security

All playlist files and mixes are stored locally on your device.

We do not access, store, or share your personal files.

### 6. Children's Privacy

Playlist Automation does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
